Our Story

A Taste of Home

Bánh Xèo Quán is a family run restaurant in Rosemead, California, specializing in crispy and savory Vietnamese crepes.

Every dish on our menu is prepared from scratch using fresh ingredients and recipes passed down through generations. From the sizzle of our signature bánh xèo to our hearty noodle soups, we cook with the same care as if you were sitting at our family table.

We believe good food brings people together. Pull up a chair — you're family here.
